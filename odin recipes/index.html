My favourite Dish

The lasagna dish has found its way to become my most favourite dish in recent times.

Its definitely a dish to try.

Ingredients
  • 1 package lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 pound shredded mozzarella
  • 1/2 pound shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 print ricotta cheese
    • Steps
    1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until drain
    2. preheat oven to 350 degrees F(175 degrees C). In a large skillet over a meduim-high heat,brown and season with salt and pepper; drain. stir in spaghetti sauce and garlic sauce and garkic and simmer 5 munites.
    3. In a meduim bowl,combine mozarella, cheddar and ricotta; stir well. in 9*10 inch pan, alternate layers of noodles,meat mixture and cheese mixture until pan is filled.
    4. Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until cheese is melted and buddy.
