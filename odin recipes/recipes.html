Ingredients
  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 eggplant, peeled and cut into 1/2-inch cubes
  • 1(28 ounce) can plum tomatoes with juice, chopped
  • 1(16 ounce)package rigatoni pasta
    • Steps
    1. heat olive oil in a large skillet over meduim heat; cook and stir garlic until fragrant, 1 to 2 minutes.
    2. Add eggplant;cook, stirring constantly, until eggplant is softened, about 5 minutes.
    3. Add tomatoes and juice;cook until sauce is slightly reduced,about 20 minutes
    4. Bring a large pot of tightly salted water to a boil.
    5. Cook rigatoni in the boiling water, stirring occasionally until cooked through but firm to the bite, about 13 minutes
    6. Drain and transfer to a serving bowl
    7. Pour saucd over pasta
